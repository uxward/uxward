## Vision

Brandon Ward is the proven design leader you bring into *any* vertical — he builds and guides the teams and the products that both delight customers and move the bottom line. Range and results, not a single-industry specialist.

The site exists to make that reputation legible and to convert it into the right role. Everything below ladders up to that vision.

---

## Strategic Goals

### Goal 1 — Land the right role

Secure the right senior design leadership role — at a company that takes design seriously, on the right terms (the mandate and the seat, not just the title). This is the primary outcome; Goals 2 and 3 exist to make it inevitable.

| # | Objective | Target |
|---|-----------|--------|
| 1.1 | Secure a senior leadership offer (Head of Design / VP Design / CDO) at a target-tier company | By mid-2027 (within ~12 months) |
| 1.2 | Reach final-round / leadership conversations with target-tier companies | ≥3 companies by Q1 2027 |
| 1.3 | Accept only a role clearing the "right terms" bar — explicit design mandate + seat at/near the exec table, measured against a written non-negotiables checklist | Zero compromises on the must-haves |

---

### Goal 2 — Make the case undeniable

Anyone who lands on the site registers a clear-thinking leader with real receipts, in seconds. This is the *conviction* prerequisite: when the right person looks, the site converts attention into belief — without which Goal 1's conversations never start.

| # | Objective | Target |
|---|-----------|--------|
| 2.1 | Homepage passes the 30-second test — reviewers can answer all four questions (clear thinking / real work / specific POV / want them in the room) | 5+ senior peers in blind review, by Q3 2026 |
| 2.2 | Hero case (Speakeazy) fully realized + supporting cases live on the 6-step template, incl. wiring Precocity into the slate | Hero + 4 supporting cases at launch |
| 2.3 | Homepage successfully hands off to the work — engaged sessions reach a case study | ≥40% of engaged sessions reach a case (once analytics are live) |

---

### Goal 3 — Be sought, not seeking

Build durable presence and reputation so the right opportunities arrive without chasing them. This is the *presence* prerequisite: it widens the top of the funnel for Goal 1 (recruiters forwarding the link; peers amplifying the writing) so Brandon is pulled toward roles rather than pushing toward them.

| # | Objective | Target |
|---|-----------|--------|
| 3.1 | Hold the quarterly writing cadence across the four "On…" sections | 4 essays/year on time; ≥28 live at launch |
| 3.2 | Generate qualified inbound that references the site (recruiter / hiring-manager outreach) | ≥6 qualified inbound conversations over 12 months |
| 3.3 | uxward.com is the canonical "Brandon Ward" result and the link recruiters forward | Ranks #1 above LinkedIn and the acting site within 6 months |
